The Best Movies According to Earl

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fast and Furious

There are many Fast and Furious movies to watch in this franchise. However, I will always be a sucker for the original. I just felt like the original was about street racing and car culture with some action thrown into the mix. Where as the new ones are all about a ton of action with some car culture thrown in. Completely different in my opinion

Rose Red

Stephen King will forever be the king of horror. His movie Rose Red was more than just making you dump the popcorn and jump off your seat at a jump scare. It had story, character development, a thrilling plot, and a gorgous scenery to keep you emerged. The pinnacle of horror in my opinion

Santa Clause

The original Santa Clause will forever hold a place in my heart around the holidays. Tim Allen as always did a great job at playing the Santa Claus. Bring comedy and family drama all into one package. This is a great Christmas story that will keep everyone entertained with just the right amount of happiness, comedy, and sadness that every holiday movie needs.
